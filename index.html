About

The Research Bazaar is a worldwide festival promoting the digital literacy emerging at the center of modern research.

During the first week of February 2016, events will be held at a number of university campuses around the globe. The aim of these events is to equip researchers from all career stages with the digital skills and tools required to do their research better, faster and smarter. A unique blend of academic and social life, ResBaz is an event unlike any academic conference you've ever been to.

To read more about the history and philosophy of ResBaz, check out these blog posts from The Research Whisperer and Software Carpentry.
